We are a family owned business, started by Pat. Pat was fishing with his daughter when she asked for some cookies, but they only had the salmon they just caught. Why don't we just make the salmon into cookies?She asked. Never one to let down his children, Pat thought long and hard about it and finally came up with the recipe. And that is how Pat's cookie shack was born. The rest is history.

All of our locations feature indoor and outdoor seating, as well as high speed WiFi and mobile charging stations. If you just came to grab a coffe on a fly, you came to the right place! If you like to lounge with a delicious cookie and coffee, you also came to the right place. This is the right place.
